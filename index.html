EXPLAINING HOW THE APP WORKS IN FOUR STEPS

01.Create Account

To create an account, you will need to provide your country, phone number, name, and email address. With the help of the number you can receive a verification code and contact the administrator, and with the help of email you can recover the password. Registration will not take you more than a couple of minutes.

02.Search hotel

To search for a hotel, you will need to select the category of accommodation, the number of people and press the search button.

03.Receiving an offer

Our smart algorithms will find the best room for you within walking distance of your location. You just have to accept the offer or reject it and wait for a new one.

04.Choice and payment

To pay for the selected accommodation and confirm the settlement, you need to make a payment. This can be done using any map of the world and even cryptocurrency. If the payment is successful, you will see “Congratulations” and you can call a taxi to the hotel.
